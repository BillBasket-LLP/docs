## Try it: Bali Electronics

<Steps>
  <Step title="Start a draft count and enter what you actually counted">
    Vikram Bali runs a physical stock count at his Service Center
    Warehouse, starting a draft reconciliation and entering the quantity he
    counted for a phone model — the system expects 1, and he counts 1 on
    the shelf.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/warehouse/reconcile-count-entered-phone.png" alt="Draft count with the counted quantity entered, matching the system total" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Post the count to finalize it">
    With no variance found this time, Vikram posts the count. BillBasket
    confirms the reconciliation is posted and the system total stays in
    sync with the shelf.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/warehouse/reconcile-posted-phone.png" alt="Reconciliation posted confirmation" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Switch to the Expiry view to write off a bad batch">
    While counting, Vikram also notices a batch of screen protectors that
    expired last month. He switches to the Expiry view, finds that batch,
    and marks it **Expired** to write it off separately from the
    reconciliation.
  </Step>
</Steps>
