## Try it: Bali Electronics

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Warehouse screen's Transfers tab and start a new transfer">
    Ahead of a repair drive, Vikram Bali opens a new stock transfer at
    **Bali Electronics**, setting the source to "In-Store" and the
    destination to "Service Center Warehouse."
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pick the products and quantities to move">
    He selects 5 units of a phone model that his technicians will need for
    repairs, then saves the transfer.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check the transfer list to confirm it went through">
    Later that day, Vikram checks the transfer list, finds the transfer by
    its number and date, and confirms its status shows the stock has moved
    to the Service Center Warehouse.
  </Step>
</Steps>
