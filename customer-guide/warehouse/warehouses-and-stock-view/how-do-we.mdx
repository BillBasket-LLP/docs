## Try it: Bali Electronics

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Warehouse and add a new location">
    Vikram Bali, owner of **Bali Electronics**, opens Warehouse and adds a
    new location, "Service Center Warehouse," with its address and phone
    number.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/warehouse/warehouse-created-phone.png" alt="Service Center Warehouse added to the Warehouses list" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a sell mode that matches how stock actually moves">
    Since he wants stock to physically arrive at the service center before
    it can be billed from there, Vikram sets the store's sell mode to
    **Transfer** rather than Direct.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the stock view and pick a location to check">
    Before sending any phones over, Vikram opens the stock view, picks
    "Service Center Warehouse" from the dropdown, and confirms it's
    currently empty — ready for the first transfer.
  </Step>
</Steps>
