For businesses that ship orders out from a warehouse, BillBasket can turn
that warehouse into a **delivery center** — a place where pending orders
wait to be dispatched, with a printed challan for every shipment. Once
stock is moving, you can also trace any individual unit by its serial or
batch number to see exactly where it's been.

<Note>
  Warehouse is an optional add-on module — turn it on from Settings →
  Add-ons (Admin PIN required) if you don't see it in your menu.
</Note>

## Dispatching from a delivery center

Pick a warehouse to act as your delivery center, and BillBasket shows you
the pending orders and invoices waiting to ship from it. Dispatch one by
printing a **Dispatch Challan** — this flips the order's status to
"dispatched." If you need another copy later, already-dispatched orders
can have their challan reprinted on demand.

## Tracing by serial or batch number

Search by serial number (for serial-tracked products — see [Tracking
serial numbers](/customer-guide/products-and-services/serial-number-tracking))
or by batch number to see that exact unit's product details, current
warehouse location, status, and full movement history. Serial and batch
search both support scanning a barcode instead of typing. You can also
print a **HUN** (Hierarchical Unit Number) traceability label for a unit.

## Try it: Bali Electronics

<Steps>
  <Step title="Pick a delivery center and find the pending order">
    A customer's phone order needs to ship from the Service Center
    Warehouse. Vikram Bali picks it as the Delivery Center and switches to
    the Incoming tab to find the pending order.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Dispatch the order and print the Dispatch Challan">
    Vikram dispatches the order, printing the Dispatch Challan. His staff
    member Deepak later needs a spare copy for the courier, so he reopens
    the same order from the Dispatched tab and reprints it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Look up a unit by serial or HUN in the Trace tab">
    A few days later, Vikram switches to the Trace tab and searches for
    that phone's serial number to confirm where it was dispatched from and
    its current status.
