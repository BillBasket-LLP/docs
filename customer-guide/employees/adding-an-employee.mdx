title: "Adding an employee"
description: "Add cashiers, salespeople, and other staff to BillBasket so every invoice is linked to who created it and sales can be tracked per employee."
---

Add an employee from **Employees → New Employee** with a name and optional
contact details. Once added, you can link sales to them for accountability
and reporting.
