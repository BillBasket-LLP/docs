Manage active devices from your account's Devices page. Revoking a device
signs it out and frees up a license slot for a new one.

## Try it: Sharma Kirana Store

<Steps>
  <Step title="Rajesh opens the Devices page">
    Rajesh Sharma signs in to his BillBasket account and opens the Devices
    page to see everything currently signed in: his Windows counter PC and
    an old Android phone he no longer uses at the shop.
  </Step>
  <Step title="He spots the old phone">
    The list shows the old phone was last active over a month ago, back
    when Suresh briefly used it for billing during a busy festival week.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Revoke the old device">
    Rajesh taps the old phone entry and selects **Sign out this device**.
    It immediately signs out and frees up a license slot.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Activate a new device with the freed slot">
    With the slot free, Rajesh can now sign in on Suresh's new work phone
    and activate BillBasket there instead.
  </Step>
</Steps>
