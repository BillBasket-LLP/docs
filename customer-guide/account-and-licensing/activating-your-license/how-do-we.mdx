## Try it: Sharma Kirana Store

<Steps>
  <Step title="Install BillBasket on the new device">
    Rajesh Sharma has just bought a second counter PC for Sharma Kirana
    Store. He installs BillBasket and, on first launch, is asked to sign in
    or activate a license.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign in with the store's BillBasket account">
    He signs in with the same BillBasket account email he used on his
    original device. BillBasket checks his plan and confirms he still has a
    free device slot available.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Let the license activate automatically">
    The new PC activates against Rajesh's plan and unlocks the full app —
    no separate license key entry needed since he's signed into the same
    account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Free up a slot if the device count is full">
    A few months later, Rajesh tries to activate a third device but his plan
    only allows two. BillBasket tells him to free up a slot first — see
    [Devices & login](/customer-guide/account-and-licensing/devices-and-login/explainer)
    to sign an old device out.
  </Step>
</Steps>
