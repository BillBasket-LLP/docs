title: "Devices & login"
description: "See which device you're signed in on, check your plan's device usage, and sign out this device from your BillBasket account."
---

BillBasket ties your subscription to your BillBasket account, and each
device you sign in on (a phone, tablet, or Windows PC) counts against your
plan's device limit. **Settings → License & Account** shows you the current
device's status and lets you sign it out when you're retiring it or handing
it to someone else.

<Note>
  BillBasket doesn't show a remote list of every other signed-in device from
  this screen — it shows the status of the device you're currently using.
  To free up a slot, sign out from the old device itself (or reset your
  account online at [Manage account](/customer-guide/account-and-licensing/activating-your-license/explainer)).
</Note>

## Understanding the fields

- **Licensed to** — your account's business name, owner name, mobile number,
  and email, shown read-only. This comes from your BillBasket account, not
  from this device, so it stays the same no matter which device you sign
  into.
- **Package** — your current plan name (for example, a trial or a paid
  package tier).
- **Trial Stores / Pro Stores** — shows how many stores your plan allows at
  each level, as "X of Y" (for example, "1 of 2"). If you're using a trial
  store and haven't reached your Pro Store limit, an **Activate this store**
  button appears — this converts the current store from trial to a paid
  Pro slot without losing any data. If your Pro Store count is already at
  its limit, activating a new store isn't possible until you free one up or
  upgrade your plan.
- **License status** — whether the current lease (BillBasket's offline proof
  that this device is entitled to run) is valid, expired, or needs
  attention. This is what lets the app keep working for a while even
  without internet.
- **Valid until / days left** — the date the current lease needs to renew
  by, and how many days remain. BillBasket renews this automatically in the
  background roughly every 6 hours while online, so you normally never see
  it lapse.
- **This device** and **Machine ID** — an identifier unique to this
  installation, useful when reporting a problem to support.
- **Renew now** — manually forces a lease renewal check, instead of waiting
  for the automatic background renewal. Useful right after fixing a network
  issue.
- **Sign out** — signs this device out of your BillBasket account and clears
  its license. Signing out *this* device is what frees up a device slot for
  a different device to sign in — there's no separate action needed on
  another device.
- **Connect this device to an existing store** (only shown if your account
  has a store that isn't set up on this device yet) — lets you pull in a
  store you've already created elsewhere instead of accidentally creating a
  duplicate.
