## Try it: Sharma Kirana Store

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Settings and go to License & Account">
    Rajesh Sharma signs in to BillBasket on his Windows counter PC and opens
    **Settings → License & Account** to check his plan.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check the Trial/Pro Store count">
    He sees "Pro Stores: 2 of 2" — his plan's two device/store slots are both
    in use, one of them an old Android phone Suresh briefly used during a
    busy festival week.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign out the old device">
    Rajesh picks up the old phone, opens **Settings → License & Account**
    there, and taps **Sign out**. It immediately signs out and frees a slot.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign in on the new device with the freed slot">
    With the slot free, Rajesh signs in with the same BillBasket account on
    Suresh's new work phone, which activates automatically against the
    freed slot.
  </Step>
</Steps>
