Your Business Profile is the identity block printed at the top and bottom
of every invoice and estimate — your business name, address, contact
details, tax ID, and (if customers pay by transfer) your bank details.
Update it anytime from **Settings → General → Business Profile**.

<Note>
  There's no logo upload on this screen today — despite older guidance
  mentioning one, the current Business Profile form covers business details
  and bank details only. If a logo option is added later, this page will be
  updated.
</Note>

## Understanding the fields

- **Business name** — required. Prints as the main heading on every invoice,
  estimate, and receipt.
- **Address** — your shop's address, shown under the business name on
  printed documents. Supports two lines.
- **Phone** — your shop's contact number, shown on invoices so customers can
  reach you about an order.
- **Email** — optional contact email, also shown on invoices.
- **Tax ID / GSTIN** — your business's tax registration number (GSTIN in
  India, or the equivalent for your country). Printed on invoices so they're
  valid for your customers' own tax records.
- **Bank name / Account number / IFSC / routing / Account holder name** — a
  separate "Bank details" section shown on invoices for customers paying by
  bank transfer. All four fields are optional, but for the bank block to be
  useful on the printed invoice, fill in all of them together — a partially
  filled set (for example, an account number with no IFSC) isn't enough for
  most customers to actually complete a transfer.

Only **Business name** is required to save; everything else can be filled
in later. Changes save to this device immediately, and — if you're signed
in to your BillBasket account — also sync to the cloud so they carry over
if you set up another device.
