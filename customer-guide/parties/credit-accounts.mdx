---
title: "Credit accounts & payment tracking"
description: "Let trusted customers buy on credit in BillBasket — set credit limits, track outstanding dues, and send payment reminders when bills come due."
---

Enable credit for a customer to bill them without full payment upfront.
BillBasket tracks their running balance, and you can record payments against
it at any time, independent of any single invoice.

## Try it: Sharma Kirana Store

<Steps>
  <Step title="Rajesh enables credit for Meena Devi">
    Meena Devi is a trusted regular, so Rajesh Sharma opens her party
    profile and turns on credit, setting a credit limit he's comfortable
    with.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Meena buys on credit">
    Meena picks up Parle-G Biscuits, Tata Salt, and Amul Milk without
    paying cash. Rajesh bills the invoice against her account, and her
    outstanding balance goes up by that amount.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Track the running balance">
    Over the following weeks, Meena buys a few more times on credit.
    Rajesh checks her party profile anytime to see her running dues total.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Record a payment">
    At month's end, Meena pays off part of what she owes. Rajesh records
    the payment against her account, independent of any single invoice,
    and her balance drops accordingly.
  </Step>
</Steps>
