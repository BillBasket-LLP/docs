---
title: "Barcode scanning (camera + hardware scanner)"
description: "Scan barcodes into a BillBasket bill or product catalog using your phone camera, a USB scanner, or a Bluetooth handheld scanner on Android and Windows."
---

BillBasket supports scanning via your phone or desktop webcam, or any
USB/Bluetooth barcode scanner that types like a keyboard. Scanning works both
while billing and while adding new products.

## Try it: Sharma Kirana Store

<Steps>
  <Step title="Suresh scans a product while billing">
    Meena Devi brings a packet of Parle-G Biscuits to the counter. Suresh
    taps the scan icon on the billing screen and uses the phone's camera to
    scan the barcode.
  </Step>
  <Step title="The item adds itself to the bill">
    BillBasket recognizes the barcode and adds Parle-G Biscuits at ₹10
    straight to Meena's invoice, no manual search needed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Rajesh uses a USB scanner at the counter PC">
    On the Windows counter PC, Rajesh Sharma has a USB barcode scanner
    plugged in. He scans Tata Salt 1kg and it behaves just like typing,
    adding the item instantly.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Scan while adding a new product">
    When Rajesh receives a new product without it yet in BillBasket, he
    opens **New Product** and scans its barcode there so it's pre-filled
    for future scans.
  </Step>
</Steps>
