## Try it: Bali Electronics

<Steps>
  <Step title="Turn on Track Serial Numbers on the product">
    Vikram Bali, owner of **Bali Electronics**, adds his "Samsung Galaxy
    M14" product and switches on **Track Serial Numbers** so every phone can
    be identified individually.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/products-and-services/serial-number-tracking/serial-toggle-on-phone.png" alt="Track Serial Numbers toggle turned on for Samsung Galaxy M14" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter each unit's serial number when you receive stock">
    When a shipment of phones arrives from his supplier, Bansal Wholesale
    Traders, Vikram opens the purchase and enters each phone's IMEI/serial
    number as he unpacks the box. BillBasket only adds the stock once every
    serial is captured.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/products-and-services/serial-number-tracking/receive-items-phone.png" alt="Receive Items screen confirming all serials captured before stock is added" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pick the exact serial being sold at billing time">
    His staff member Deepak bills a walk-in customer for one Galaxy M14. At
    the billing screen, BillBasket shows the list of in-stock serials for
    that phone — Deepak picks the exact unit being handed over, or scans its
    barcode/serial directly.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/products-and-services/serial-number-tracking/pos-serial-selected-phone.png" alt="Picking an in-stock serial number while billing at POS" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Process a return against the same serial">
    Later, if that same customer returns the phone for a defect, Vikram
    looks up the sale and processes the return against that specific serial
    number, so stock and warranty records stay tied to the exact unit.
  </Step>
</Steps>
