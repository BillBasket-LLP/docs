---
title: "Adding a service"
description: "Add a billable service like labor, repair, or installation to BillBasket alongside your products, with its own price, tax rate, and unit of measure."
---

Services work like products for billing purposes but skip stock tracking.
Add them from **Products → New Service** with a name, price, tax rate, and
unit of measure.

## Try it: Sharma Kirana Store

<Steps>
  <Step title="Rajesh starts offering home delivery">
    Sharma Kirana Store has started offering home delivery for large
    orders. Rajesh Sharma opens **Products → New Service** to add it as a
    billable item.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter name, price, and tax rate">
    He names it "Home Delivery," sets a flat price, and picks the
    applicable tax rate.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set the unit of measure">
    Since delivery is charged per trip rather than by weight or count, he
    sets the unit of measure to "service" instead of a product-style unit.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Bill it alongside products">
    When Meena Devi orders groceries for delivery, Rajesh adds "Home
    Delivery" to her invoice alongside her Parle-G Biscuits and Amul Milk —
    no stock is deducted for the service line.
  </Step>
</Steps>
