---
title: "Units of measure (UOM)"
description: "Set how a BillBasket product or service is sold and measured — pieces, kilograms, liters, meters, or custom units — and switch UOM per invoice line."
---

Every product or service has a unit of measure (UOM) — for example piece,
kg, or liter. BillBasket ships with common built-in units and lets you add
your own from **Settings → Units**.
