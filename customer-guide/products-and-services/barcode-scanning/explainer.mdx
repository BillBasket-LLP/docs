BillBasket supports scanning barcodes on every platform, but the setup
differs by device:

### On your phone (built-in camera)

On Android and iOS, the phone's own camera scans barcodes live — just tap
the scan icon while billing or adding a product. This works out of the box,
no setup needed.

### On Windows PC (USB scanner or barcode printer)

- **USB/Bluetooth barcode scanner** — plug in or pair a handheld barcode
  scanner and it works immediately, with zero configuration. It acts like a
  keyboard: scanning a code just "types" it into whatever field is focused,
  the same way it would on a phone with a connected scanner.
- **Webcam scanning** — Windows can also scan using a connected webcam, but
  this is an experimental, opt-in feature (off by default) since it's less
  reliable than a dedicated scanner.
- **USB barcode/label printer** — a USB label or receipt printer is
  installed like any normal Windows printer (via its own driver) and then
  selected from the print dialog — see [Printing & sharing
  bills](/customer-guide/daily-billing/printing-and-sharing/explainer).

Scanning works both while billing and while adding new products.

## Understanding the fields

In **Settings → Hardware & Connectivity → Camera**:

- **Scan barcodes with camera (Experimental)** — a toggle, off by
  default, that turns on webcam-based scanning on Windows. When it's
  off, every camera/scan-many icon across the app is hidden entirely
  (this doesn't affect a USB or Bluetooth barcode scanner, which always
  works since it behaves like a keyboard).
- **Camera picker** — only appears once the toggle above is on. Lets
  you choose a specific webcam if the PC has more than one connected;
  if you don't pick one, BillBasket just uses the system default.

See [Hardware & connectivity
settings](/customer-guide/settings/hardware-and-connectivity/explainer) for the
full settings screen.
