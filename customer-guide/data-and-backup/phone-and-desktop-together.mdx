---
title: "Using BillBasket on phone + desktop together"
description: "Pair an Android phone with your Windows counter machine so BillBasket data syncs between them and you can bill from either device on the same store."
---

Your Android phone can connect to your Windows BillBasket over your shop's
Wi-Fi, so staff can bill from a phone while everything stays in sync with the
counter machine — no internet required, just the same local network.

## Try it: Sharma Kirana Store

<Steps>
  <Step title="Rajesh sets up the Windows counter as the main device">
    Rajesh Sharma runs BillBasket on the Windows PC at the counter of
    Sharma Kirana Store — this holds the main store data.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Suresh connects his phone over Wi-Fi">
    Suresh, the billing staff, opens BillBasket on his Android phone while
    connected to the shop's Wi-Fi and pairs it with Rajesh's counter PC.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Suresh bills a customer from his phone">
    During a busy hour, Suresh bills Meena Devi's purchase directly from
    his phone while walking the store aisles, instead of making her wait
    at the counter.
  </Step>
  <Step title="The sale syncs back to the counter instantly">
    The invoice Suresh created appears on Rajesh's Windows counter PC right
    away, kept in sync over the local network — no internet connection
    needed.
  </Step>
</Steps>
