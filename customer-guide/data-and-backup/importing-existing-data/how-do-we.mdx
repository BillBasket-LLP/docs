## Try it: Sharma Kirana Store

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Data Import/Export">
    Rajesh Sharma has a spreadsheet of his existing products from his old
    paper ledger. He opens **Settings → Data Import/Export** on his
    Windows PC to bring it into BillBasket.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Download the template">
    He downloads the product import template first, so his columns —
    name, price, tax rate, opening stock — line up with what BillBasket
    expects.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill in the template">
    Rajesh fills in his products, including Parle-G Biscuits at ₹10, Tata
    Salt 1kg at ₹28, and Amul Milk 500ml at ₹32, along with their opening
    stock counts.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload the completed file">
    He uploads the completed file. BillBasket reads it and adds all the
    products at once, ready to bill against immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>
