## Try it: Sharma Kirana Store

<Steps>
  <Step title="Set up the Windows counter as the host">
    Rajesh Sharma runs BillBasket on the Windows PC at the counter of
    Sharma Kirana Store — this holds the main store data.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pair a phone over Wi-Fi">
    Suresh, the billing staff, opens BillBasket on his Android phone
    while connected to the shop's Wi-Fi and pairs it with Rajesh's
    counter PC.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Bill a customer from the phone">
    During a busy hour, Suresh bills Meena Devi's purchase directly from
    his phone while walking the store aisles, instead of making her wait
    at the counter.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Watch it sync back to the counter instantly">
    The invoice Suresh created appears on Rajesh's Windows counter PC
    right away, kept in sync over the local network — no internet
    connection needed.
  </Step>
</Steps>
