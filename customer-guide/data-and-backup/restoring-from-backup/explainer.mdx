---
title: "Restoring from backup"
description: "Restore your BillBasket store from a previous backup — either the full database or individual tables like products, customers, or invoices."
---

Restore from **Settings → Backup & Restore → Restore**. You can restore a
full snapshot or pick specific tables/data to restore.

## Understanding the fields

- **Backup to restore** — picked from the list of previous encrypted
  backups tied to your account and store; you can't restore a backup
  belonging to a different store or account.
- **Restore scope: full vs. per-table** — a full restore replaces
  everything with that backup's snapshot. Picking specific tables instead
  (for example, just Customers) only touches that data, leaving
  everything else — like invoices created since the backup — untouched.
  Use the narrower option when you're recovering from a mistake in one
  area, not rolling the whole store back in time.

<Note>
  Restoring the full snapshot rolls back everything to that backup's
  point in time — any sales or changes made after that backup was taken
  are lost unless you restore just the specific table you need instead.
</Note>
