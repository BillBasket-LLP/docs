## Try it: Sharma Kirana Store

<Steps>
  <Step title="Reinstall and sign back in">
    After his Windows PC is reset for a repair, Rajesh Sharma reinstalls
    BillBasket and signs back in to Sharma Kirana Store's account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open Restore and pick a backup">
    He goes to **Settings → Backup & Restore → Restore** and sees the
    list of previous encrypted backups tied to his store.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Restore the full snapshot">
    Rajesh picks last night's backup and chooses to restore the full
    snapshot, bringing back all products, parties like Meena Devi, and
    invoice history.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Restore just one table if needed">
    Another time, Rajesh only needs to recover the Customers table after
    an accidental bulk edit — he selects just that table from the same
    backup instead of restoring everything.
  </Step>
</Steps>
