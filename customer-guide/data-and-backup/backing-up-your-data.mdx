---
title: "Backing up your data"
description: "Create manual and scheduled encrypted backups of your BillBasket store data to local storage or the cloud, and verify a backup completed successfully."
---

Create a backup from **Settings → Backup & Restore**. Backups are
encrypted and tied to your account and store, so only you can restore
them.

## Understanding the fields

- **Back up now** — runs a manual, one-off backup immediately, regardless
  of the schedule setting below.
- **Schedule frequency** — **Off**, **Daily**, or **Weekly**. This
  controls whether BillBasket also takes a backup automatically in the
  background; it's independent of "Back up now," so turning scheduling
  off doesn't stop you from backing up manually whenever you want.
- **Backup list / timestamp** — each completed backup is listed with when
  it ran, so you can confirm the most recent one actually succeeded
  before you rely on it.

## Try it: Sharma Kirana Store

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Backup & Restore">
    Before the busy festival season, Rajesh Sharma opens **Settings →
    Backup & Restore** on his Windows counter PC to make sure his data is
    safe.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Run a manual backup">
    He taps **Back up now**. BillBasket encrypts the store's data —
    products, parties like Meena Devi, invoices, and more — and saves it,
    tied to his BillBasket account and store.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify the backup completed">
    Rajesh checks the backup list and sees a new entry timestamped just
    now, confirming it finished successfully.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Turn on scheduled backups">
    He sets the schedule to **Daily** so BillBasket keeps backing up
    automatically going forward, without him needing to remember.
  </Step>
</Steps>
