## Try it: Sharma Kirana Store

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Settings → Language">
    Suresh, who handles billing at **Sharma Kirana Store**, is more
    comfortable reading Hindi than English. Rajesh Sharma opens **Settings**
    and taps the **Language** row.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pick a language from the list">
    Rajesh selects Hindi from the list of 15+ supported Indian languages
    (plus English and a few others). The list closes and the change applies
    right away.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm the app switched">
    BillBasket applies the change across the whole app immediately — menus,
    buttons, and labels now show in Hindi for Suresh to use comfortably. The
    **Language** row's subtitle also updates to show "Hindi" as the current
    choice.
  </Step>
  <Step title="(Optional) Switch the theme too">
    If the shop's PC is hard to read in a bright window, Rajesh taps the
    **Theme** row in Settings and picks **Dark** instead of **System**. He
    can switch back to **System** any time so BillBasket follows the
    device's own light/dark setting again.
  </Step>
</Steps>
