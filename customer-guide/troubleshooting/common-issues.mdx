---
title: "Common issues"
description: "Quick fixes for common BillBasket problems — printer not detected, barcode scanner not working, sync stuck, license activation errors, and more."
---

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="BillBasket won't open">
    Restart your device and try again. If it still won't open, contact
    support — see [Contacting
    support](/customer-guide/troubleshooting/contacting-support).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Printer isn't printing">
    Check the printer is powered on and connected, and that it's selected
    under printer settings.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Barcode scanner isn't detected">
    Reconnect the scanner and make sure it's in keyboard-emulation mode; for
    camera scanning, check camera permission is granted.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
