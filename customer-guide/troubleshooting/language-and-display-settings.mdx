title: "Language & display settings"
description: "Change the BillBasket app language and switch between light, dark, or system theme from Settings so the app fits your shop and your local language."
---

BillBasket lets you pick the language the whole app is shown in, and choose
whether it looks light, dark, or matches your device's own setting. Both
are available from the main **Settings** screen, and both apply
immediately — no restart needed.

<Frame>
  ![Settings Theme page with System, Light, and Dark appearance options](/images/troubleshooting/theme-settings-windows.png)
</Frame>

## Understanding the fields

<Note>
  These are the two fields on the Settings screen itself. There's no
  separate font-size or text-scale setting inside BillBasket — the app
  follows your device's own text-size setting instead (see below).
</Note>

- **Language** — Tap this row to open a list of every language BillBasket
  supports: English plus 15+ Indian languages (Hindi, Bengali, Marathi,
  Telugu, Tamil, Gujarati, Kannada, Odia, Malayalam, Punjabi, Assamese,
  Nepali, Mizo) and a few others (Spanish, French, Arabic). Pick one and
  every menu, button, and label across the app switches immediately. The
  row's subtitle always shows your current choice, so you can tell at a
  glance what's active without opening the list.
- **Theme** — Tap this row to choose **System**, **Light**, or **Dark**.
  **System** follows your device's own light/dark setting and switches
  automatically if that changes (for example, at sunset on a phone with
  auto dark mode). **Light** and **Dark** lock BillBasket to that
  appearance regardless of what your device is set to. On phone and
  tablet, there's also a quick icon in the top-right corner of Settings
  that cycles System → Light → Dark → System in one tap, as a shortcut to
  the same setting.

<Note>
  Text size isn't a BillBasket setting — it follows the font-size setting
  in your phone's or PC's own system settings. If everything in BillBasket
  looks too small or too large, change the text size for your whole device
  and BillBasket will follow (within reasonable limits, so layouts don't
  break).
</Note>

## Try it: Sharma Kirana Store

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Settings → Language">
    Suresh, who handles billing at **Sharma Kirana Store**, is more
    comfortable reading Hindi than English. Rajesh Sharma opens **Settings**
    and taps the **Language** row.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pick a language from the list">
    Rajesh selects Hindi from the list of 15+ supported Indian languages
    (plus English and a few others). The list closes and the change applies
    right away.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm the app switched">
    BillBasket applies the change across the whole app immediately — menus,
    buttons, and labels now show in Hindi for Suresh to use comfortably. The
    **Language** row's subtitle also updates to show "Hindi" as the current
    choice.
  </Step>
  <Step title="(Optional) Switch the theme too">
    If the shop's PC is hard to read in a bright window, Rajesh taps the
    **Theme** row in Settings and picks **Dark** instead of **System**. He
    can switch back to **System** any time so BillBasket follows the
    device's own light/dark setting again.
  </Step>
</Steps>
