---
title: "Language & display settings"
description: "Change the BillBasket app language, font size, theme, and receipt layout anytime from Settings so the app fits your shop and your local language."
---

Change your language from **Settings → Language**. BillBasket supports
English and 15+ Indian languages, and applies your choice across the whole
app immediately.

## Try it: Sharma Kirana Store

<Steps>
  <Step title="Suresh prefers Hindi">
    Suresh, who handles billing at Sharma Kirana Store, is more
    comfortable reading Hindi than English. Rajesh Sharma opens **Settings
    → Language** to change it for him.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pick a language from the list">
    Rajesh selects Hindi from the list of 15+ supported Indian languages.
  </Step>
  <Step title="The app switches immediately">
    BillBasket applies the change across the whole app right away — menus,
    buttons, and labels now show in Hindi for Suresh to use comfortably.
  </Step>
</Steps>
