---

**Settings → General** holds the everyday app-wide options that don't fit
under a specific feature — language and appearance, sensible defaults for
new bills and customers, your business profile, and information about the
app itself.

## Understanding the fields

- **Language** — changes the app's display language anytime. See
  [Language & display settings](/customer-guide/troubleshooting/language-and-display-settings)
  for the full list of supported languages and how to switch.
- **Theme (System / Light / Dark)** — switches the app's appearance.
  System follows your device's own light/dark setting automatically.
- **Date format** — a dropdown of date patterns, with a live preview
  showing "Today reads as …" using your current selection. This isn't just
  cosmetic: it also affects how BillBasket reads numeric batch labels off
  scanned products — a label like `03/04` is read differently depending on
  whether your format is day-first or month-first. Pick the format that
  matches how your own batch numbers are written, and note it applies
  app-wide, not per-screen.
- **Default credit limit** — the most a customer can owe you on account
  before BillBasket blocks a further credit (unpaid) sale to them. This
  value only sets the *default* applied automatically to newly added
  customers — you can still raise, lower, or clear the limit on any
  individual customer afterward. Leave it blank or 0 for no default limit.
- **Performance Log** (toggle) — for troubleshooting only; when on, it
  records slow database queries and any that wait on a lock, useful if
  BillBasket (or your support contact) needs to investigate slowness with
  several devices billing at once. A **View log** button appears once it's
  been recording. It adds no overhead when off, so leave it off for normal
  day-to-day use unless asked to turn it on.
- **Business Profile** — manage your business's name, address, contact
  details, and tax ID shown on invoices. See
  [Managing your store profile](/customer-guide/account-and-licensing/managing-your-store-profile)
  for the full walkthrough.
- **About BillBasket** — shows the app version you're currently running,
  lets you check for updates, and lists open-source licenses used inside
  BillBasket. Check this first if you're reporting a problem — support will
  usually ask for your version number.

## Try it: Sharma Kirana Store

<Steps>
  <Step title="Set the date format">
    Rajesh Sharma writes his batch numbers day-first, so he opens
    **Settings → General → Defaults** and sets the date format to match,
    checking the "Today reads as" preview to make sure batch labels get
    read correctly.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set a default credit limit">
    Many of his regular customers buy on credit, so he sets a default
    customer credit limit of ₹2,000 in the same Defaults section — new
    customers now start with that limit automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check the app version">
    Before calling support about a sync question, Rajesh opens **Settings →
    General → About BillBasket** to note down his current app version.
  </Step>
</Steps>
