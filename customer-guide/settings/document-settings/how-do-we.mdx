## Try it: James Spa

<Steps>
  <Step title="Add a signature and bank details">
    James Fernandes opens **Settings → Documents → Invoice Template &
    Branding**, uploads a photo of his signature, and fills in his bank
    account details so clients paying by transfer can see them on the
    invoice.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create a separate estimate series">
    He then opens **Settings → Documents → Invoice Numbering** and creates a
    second series with the prefix `EST-` starting at 1, and marks it as the
    default series for estimates, so his estimates are numbered separately
    from his invoices.
  </Step>
</Steps>
