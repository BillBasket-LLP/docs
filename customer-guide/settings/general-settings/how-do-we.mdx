## Try it: Sharma Kirana Store

<Steps>
  <Step title="Set the date format">
    Rajesh Sharma writes his batch numbers day-first, so he opens
    **Settings → General → Defaults** and sets the date format to match,
    checking the "Today reads as" preview to make sure batch labels get
    read correctly.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set a default credit limit">
    Many of his regular customers buy on credit, so he sets a default
    customer credit limit of ₹2,000 in the same Defaults section — new
    customers now start with that limit automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check the app version">
    Before calling support about a sync question, Rajesh opens **Settings →
    General → About BillBasket** to note down his current app version.
  </Step>
</Steps>
