**Settings → Add-ons** and the surrounding system options cover the
optional modules included in your plan, your store's weekly schedule,
Windows startup behavior, and account-level settings like your Admin PIN
and license.

## Understanding the fields

### Add-ons

- **Module switch** (one per module — e.g. Employee HR, Warehouse,
  Appointments/Services) — turns a whole optional section of the app on or
  off for your store. A module only shows a working switch if it's
  **included in your plan**; otherwise it's shown unavailable. Since
  turning a module on or off adds or removes whole sections of the app's
  navigation, this action requires your **Admin PIN** if one is set. See
  [Admin PIN and permissions](/customer-guide/employees/admin-pin-and-permissions/explainer).
  Turning on **Employee HR** or **Warehouse** unlocks the corresponding
  section of this guide.

### Non-working Days

- **Weekly off-day chips (Mon–Sun)** — tap a day to mark it as a recurring
  weekly closure (for example, every Sunday). This only sets your
  **regular weekly** schedule. Specific one-off holidays (like a festival
  closure on a particular date) are a separate system, managed on the
  Employee HR module's Holidays page — see
  [Holidays](/customer-guide/employee-hr/holidays/explainer). The two don't share data:
  marking a weekly off-day here doesn't create a Holidays entry, and vice
  versa.

### Startup & Background

Windows only — this section doesn't appear on Android.

- **Run at login** — starts BillBasket automatically when the PC turns on.
- **Minimize to tray** (on by default) — keeps BillBasket running quietly
  in the background when you close the window, instead of fully exiting.
  These two toggles are independent — you can turn on minimize-to-tray
  without run-at-login (BillBasket just won't fully quit once you've
  started it manually), though together they're what keeps BillBasket
  ready at the counter without ever needing to be opened manually.

### Admin PIN

- **Enable Admin PIN** (toggle) — turning this on prompts you to set a
  6-digit PIN that then protects sensitive actions like Settings,
  Employees, and product edits. Turning it off requires confirming the
  current PIN first.
- **Change PIN** (only shown once enabled) — set a new 6-digit PIN,
  replacing the old one.
- **Lock now** — immediately re-locks the admin-protected areas without
  waiting for the app to lock itself, useful right before you hand the
  counter to staff.
- **Forgot PIN?** — a reset path tied to your BillBasket account, for when
  the PIN is lost.

See [Admin PIN and permissions](/customer-guide/employees/admin-pin-and-permissions/explainer)
for the full guide to what the PIN protects.

### License & Account

Manage your BillBasket license and see this device's status. See
[Activating your license](/customer-guide/account-and-licensing/activating-your-license/explainer)
and [Devices and login](/customer-guide/account-and-licensing/devices-and-login/explainer)
for details.

### AI Assistant

- **Master switch** — turns BillBasket's built-in AI assistant on or off.
  Everything else below only appears once this is on.
- **Where should AI run? (On this device / Cloud / Automatic)** — On
  Windows, "on this device" runs locally via Ollama; on Android it depends
  on whether the phone can run it (checked automatically) — a phone that
  can't run AI locally falls back to Cloud. Choosing Automatic lets
  BillBasket pick the best option for the device it's running on, rather
  than you having to know which to choose. The engine status chip at the
  top of the screen shows whether it's currently set up and ready, or
  needs setup.
- **Advanced (Windows only, collapsed by default)** — technical detail like
  the Ollama connection address and model name; most shop owners never
  need to open this.
