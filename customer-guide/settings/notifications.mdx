**Settings → Notifications** controls how BillBasket sends invoices,
receipts, and reminders to your customers, and lets you customize exactly
what those messages say.

### Notification Channels

Turn on or off the channels BillBasket can use to send invoices, receipts,
and payment reminders to customers: WhatsApp, SMS, Email, or
Copy-to-Clipboard (for pasting into whichever app you prefer manually). You
can also pick a default channel, so BillBasket uses it automatically
without asking each time you send something.

### Message Templates

Edit the wording of the messages sent to customers — invoices, receipts,
payment reminders, and credit notes each have their own template. Templates
support auto-filled tags like `{customer_name}`, `{amount}`, and `{date}`,
which BillBasket replaces with the real values when the message is sent, so
you only need to write the wording once.
