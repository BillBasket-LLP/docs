## Try it: Sharma Kirana Store

<Steps>
  <Step title="Turn on WhatsApp and set it as default">
    Rajesh Sharma opens **Settings → Notifications → Notification
    Channels** and turns on WhatsApp, setting it as the default channel
    since most of his customers use it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customize the reminder message">
    He then opens **Message Templates**, taps the payment reminder
    template, and adds a friendly line — keeping the `{customer_name}` and
    `{amount}` tags in place so each customer's reminder is still
    personalized automatically.
  </Step>
</Steps>
