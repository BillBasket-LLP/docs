**Settings → Notifications** controls how BillBasket sends invoices,
receipts, and reminders to your customers, and lets you customize exactly
what those messages say.

## Understanding the fields

### Notification Channels

- **WhatsApp / SMS / Email** — toggles turning each channel on or off for
  sending customer messages.
- **Copy to clipboard** — always on and can't be turned off; it's the
  fallback that lets you paste a message into whichever app you prefer
  manually, even if you don't use any of the other channels.
- **Set as default** — appears only next to a channel that's already turned
  on and isn't already the default. Picking a default means BillBasket uses
  that channel automatically when sending, without asking you to choose
  each time. Only one channel can be default; setting a new one replaces
  the old default.

### Message Templates

One editable template per business moment — invoice reminder, payment
receipt, refund sent, credit note, and (if the Appointments/Services add-on
is on) appointment confirmation and appointment reminder.

- **Name** — an internal label for the template, not shown to customers.
- **Subject** — used for channels that show a subject line (like email);
  ignored by channels that don't (like WhatsApp).
- **Body** — the actual message text sent to the customer.
- **Active** — turns that specific template off without affecting the
  channels themselves; an inactive template is skipped when its triggering
  event happens.
- **Tag tokens** (e.g. `{customer_name}`, `{amount}`, `{date}`) — placeholder
  tags you insert into Subject or Body; BillBasket fills in the real value
  automatically when the message is sent. Tags insert wherever your cursor
  last was — in the Subject field or the Body field — so click into the
  field you want the tag added to before tapping a tag.
- **Restore default** — resets that one template's wording back to
  BillBasket's built-in version, without touching your other templates.

<Note>
  Appointment confirmation and appointment reminder templates only appear
  once the Appointments/Services add-on is turned on — see
  [Add-ons & system settings](/customer-guide/settings/add-ons-and-system/explainer).
</Note>
