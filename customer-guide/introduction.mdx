---
title: "What is BillBasket?"
description: "BillBasket is offline-first billing, inventory, and business management software for Indian shops — from creating invoices to tracking stock and dues."
---

BillBasket is billing and inventory software built for small shops. You create
invoices, track stock, manage customers and suppliers, and see how your
business is doing — all from one app on your computer or phone.

<Tip>
  BillBasket works completely **offline**. You don't need the internet to
  bill a customer, add stock, or look up a report. See [Understanding
  offline-first](/customer-guide/getting-started/offline-first/explainer) for what that
  means day to day.
</Tip>

## Windows and Android, together

BillBasket runs on a Windows computer at the counter and on Android phones for
staff on the move — the same store, the same data, working together. See
[Using BillBasket on phone + desktop
together](/customer-guide/data-and-backup/phone-and-desktop-together/explainer).
