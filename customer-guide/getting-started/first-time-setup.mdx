The first time you open BillBasket, it walks you through a short setup so the
app matches your store and the way you work.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Store profile">
    Enter your store or business name, and any details you want to appear on
    printed bills (address, phone number, GSTIN/tax ID where applicable).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose your language">
    BillBasket is available in English and 15+ Indian languages. Pick the one
    your staff is most comfortable with — you can change this anytime in
    **Settings**. See [Language & display
    settings](/customer-guide/troubleshooting/language-and-display-settings).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set an Admin PIN">
    Choose a 6-digit PIN. This protects sensitive areas like Settings,
    Employees, and product editing so staff can bill customers without being
    able to change store setup. See [Admin PIN &
    permissions](/customer-guide/employees/admin-pin-and-permissions).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Activate your license">
    If your BillBasket plan requires activation, sign in or enter your
    activation details. See [Activating your
    license](/customer-guide/account-and-licensing/activating-your-license).
  </Step>
</Steps>

Once setup is done, you land on your [dashboard](/customer-guide/reports-and-dashboard/understanding-your-dashboard) — from there you're ready to [create your first invoice](/customer-guide/daily-billing/creating-an-invoice).
