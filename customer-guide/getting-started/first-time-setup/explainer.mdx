The first time you open BillBasket, a short setup wizard walks you through
a few screens so the app matches your store, your country's tax rules, and
the way you work. Once it's done, you land straight on your
[dashboard](/customer-guide/reports-and-dashboard/understanding-your-dashboard/explainer).

<Note>
  If your account already has a store registered to it (for example, you're
  adding a second device), BillBasket offers to open that existing store
  instead of creating a new one — you'll only be asked to set a PIN for this
  device.
</Note>

## Understanding the fields

**Country** — sets which tax system and currency your store uses (for
example, GST + ₹ for India, VAT + ৳ for Bangladesh, VAT + रू for Nepal).
This choice is used immediately to pick the right label for the tax field
below, and to seed your store's starting tax rates — changing country later
isn't a simple edit, so pick the country you actually trade in.

**Language** — the display language for the whole app. You can change this
anytime afterward in Settings, so it's safe to pick your best guess now.

**Store/Business name** — printed on every bill; if you signed in with an
account that already has a business name on file, this is pre-filled for
you but stays editable per store.

**Address, phone, email** — optional business details that also appear on
printed bills. The phone number is formatted according to the country you
picked in step 1.

**Tax ID** — the field label changes based on your country (GSTIN for
India, PAN/VAT Number for Nepal, BIN for Bangladesh, and so on) — this is
why country is asked first. Leaving it blank is fine; you can fill it in
later from Settings.

**Admin PIN (6 digits, entered twice)** — a separate PIN from your regular
store/login PIN. Once set, it locks **Settings**, **Employees**, and
product add/edit/delete behind this PIN, so staff can bill customers
without being able to change store setup. Both PIN fields must match
exactly or setup won't proceed — see [Admin PIN &
permissions](/customer-guide/employees/admin-pin-and-permissions/explainer).

**Activate your license** — only relevant if you signed in with a
BillBasket account rather than starting a free trial; if your plan already
has a store registered, adopting that existing store skips straight to the
PIN step since your business name, country, and currency are inherited
from your account.
