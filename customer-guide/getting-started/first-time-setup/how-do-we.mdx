## Try it: Sharma Kirana Store

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose your country and language">
    Rajesh Sharma picks **India** so BillBasket applies GST and ₹, and sets
    the display language to Hindi since that's what his staff are most
    comfortable with.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your store details">
    He enters "Sharma Kirana Store" as the business name, along with his
    shop's address and phone number, so they print correctly on every bill.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your tax ID">
    Since Rajesh picked India, the field is labeled **GSTIN** — he enters his
    shop's GSTIN. If he didn't have one handy, he could leave it blank and
    add it later from Settings.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set your Admin PIN">
    Rajesh sets a 6-digit Admin PIN and enters it again to confirm, so later
    only he can get into Settings, Employees, and product editing — his
    staff member Suresh can keep billing without needing this PIN.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Once setup is done, Rajesh lands on his
[dashboard](/customer-guide/reports-and-dashboard/understanding-your-dashboard/explainer)
— from there he's ready to [create his first
invoice](/customer-guide/daily-billing/creating-an-invoice/explainer).
