## Try it: Sharma Kirana Store

<Steps>
  <Step title="Keep billing through an outage">
    When the internet goes down at **Sharma Kirana Store** one afternoon,
    Rajesh Sharma keeps creating invoices, adding stock, and looking up
    reports exactly as before — nothing about billing depends on being
    online.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check what's paused instead of blocked">
    Rajesh notices only a couple of things wait for the connection to
    return: his license check and the optional cloud backup sync. Neither
    one stops him from serving customers.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm the data stays local and safe">
    Once the internet is back, Rajesh confirms nothing was lost — every
    invoice he created offline was already saved, encrypted, on his own
    computer the whole time.
  </Step>
</Steps>
