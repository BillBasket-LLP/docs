---
title: "Customizing the dashboard"
description: "Show, hide, and reorder the BillBasket home-screen tiles for sales, stock, dues, and expenses so the dashboard matches how you run your shop."
---

Tap the gear icon on your dashboard to enter edit mode, then drag tiles to
reorder them or toggle which ones are visible. On mobile, long-press a tile
instead.

## Try it: Sharma Kirana Store

<Steps>
  <Step title="Rajesh wants dues front and center">
    Rajesh Sharma cares most about tracking customer dues, like what Meena
    Devi still owes, so he wants that tile at the top of his Windows
    dashboard. He taps the gear icon to enter edit mode.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Drag the dues tile to the top">
    He drags the "Outstanding Dues" tile above the sales tile so it's the
    first thing he sees.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Hide a tile he doesn't use">
    Rajesh doesn't track expenses closely day-to-day, so he toggles off
    the expenses tile to reduce clutter.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Do the same on his phone">
    On his Android phone, Rajesh long-presses a tile instead of using the
    gear icon, and reorders his dashboard the same way there.
  </Step>
</Steps>
