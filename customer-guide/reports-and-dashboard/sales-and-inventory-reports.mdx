title: "Sales/inventory reports"
description: "Run detailed BillBasket reports on sales, purchases, stock, and customer dues, filter by date range, product, or customer, and export to PDF or Excel."
---

The Reports section lets you break sales down by date range, product,
customer, or employee, and see stock and dues reports in detail. Reports
can be exported or printed.

## Understanding the fields

- **Date range** — the reporting window; every report is scoped to this
  first, so changing it re-runs every filter below against the new
  window.
- **Product / category filter** — narrows a sales or stock report down to
  a specific item or category, layered on top of the date range.
- **Customer / employee filter** — narrows a report to one customer's
  purchase history or one employee's sales, useful for checking dues or
  performance individually rather than store-wide.
- **Export format (PDF/Excel)** — PDF is best for printing or sharing a
  read-only copy; Excel is best when you want to re-filter or calculate
  further outside BillBasket.

## Try it: Sharma Kirana Store

<Steps>
  <Step title="Pull a week's sales report">
    Every Sunday, Rajesh Sharma opens the Reports section and filters
    sales for the past week to see how Sharma Kirana Store performed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Break it down by product">
    He filters the same report by product and notices Tata Salt sold
    much faster than usual this week.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check the stock report">
    Switching to the stock report, Rajesh confirms Tata Salt is now
    running low, well below his usual reorder point.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Export and place a reorder">
    He exports the report to Excel to double-check the numbers, then
    places a fresh order with Bansal Wholesale Traders before he runs
    out.
  </Step>
</Steps>
