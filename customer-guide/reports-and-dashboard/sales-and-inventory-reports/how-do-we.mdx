## Try it: Sharma Kirana Store

<Steps>
  <Step title="Pull a week's sales report">
    Every Sunday, Rajesh Sharma opens the Reports section and filters
    sales for the past week to see how Sharma Kirana Store performed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Break it down by product">
    He filters the same report by product and notices Tata Salt sold
    much faster than usual this week.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check the stock report">
    Switching to the stock report, Rajesh confirms Tata Salt is now
    running low, well below his usual reorder point.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Export and place a reorder">
    He exports the report to Excel to double-check the numbers, then
    places a fresh order with Bansal Wholesale Traders before he runs
    out.
  </Step>
</Steps>
