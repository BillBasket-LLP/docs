The Reports section lets you break sales down by date range, product,
customer, or employee, and see stock and dues reports in detail. Reports
can be exported or printed.

## Understanding the fields

- **Date range** — the reporting window; every report is scoped to this
  first, so changing it re-runs every filter below against the new
  window.
- **Product / category filter** — narrows a sales or stock report down to
  a specific item or category, layered on top of the date range.
- **Customer / employee filter** — narrows a report to one customer's
  purchase history or one employee's sales, useful for checking dues or
  performance individually rather than store-wide.
- **Export format (PDF/Excel)** — PDF is best for printing or sharing a
  read-only copy; Excel is best when you want to re-filter or calculate
  further outside BillBasket.
