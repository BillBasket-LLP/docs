Tap the gear icon on your dashboard to enter edit mode, then drag tiles to
reorder them or toggle which ones are visible. On mobile, long-press a tile
instead. Your layout is remembered per store, so it stays the way you left it
next time you open BillBasket.

## Understanding the fields

- **Arrange / gear icon (Windows and tablet)** — enters edit mode on the
  wide-screen dashboard, where you drag the real cards into a new order or
  toggle any of them off directly on the dashboard itself.
- **Long-press a tile (phone)** — since dragging cards around doesn't work
  well on a small phone screen, long-pressing a tile instead opens a menu
  with two options:
  - **Hide** — removes just that tile.
  - **Rearrange widgets** — opens the full reorder sheet described below.
- **Rearrange widgets sheet** — a scrollable list of your currently visible
  blocks, each with a drag handle to reorder it and a hide icon to remove it,
  followed by an **Add widget** section listing any hidden blocks so you can
  bring them back.
- **Reset layout** — inside the rearrange sheet, this restores the original
  default order and un-hides every block. It doesn't just reorder — it also
  makes every previously hidden tile visible again, so use it if your
  dashboard has gotten too customized to find something.

<Note>
  Your show/hide and order choices are saved per store (not per device), but
  each device you're logged into keeps its own copy of the layout — so
  reordering the dashboard on your Windows PC won't rearrange it on your
  phone.
</Note>

**How show/hide and reorder relate to each other**: hiding a tile removes it
from the visible list but doesn't forget its position — if you show it again
later, BillBasket places it back near where it was (or at the end, if it's
new). Reordering only affects the tiles you can currently see; a hidden tile
has no position to drag until you bring it back into view.
