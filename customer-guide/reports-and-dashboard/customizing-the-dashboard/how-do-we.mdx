## Try it: Sharma Kirana Store

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open edit mode on the Windows dashboard">
    Rajesh Sharma cares most about tracking customer dues, like what Meena
    Devi still owes, so he wants that tile at the top of his Windows
    dashboard. He taps the gear icon to enter edit mode.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Drag the Receivables tile to the top">
    He drags the "Receivables" tile above the sales tile so it's the
    first thing he sees.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Hide a tile he doesn't use">
    Rajesh doesn't track returns closely day-to-day, so he toggles off
    the Return Stats tile to reduce clutter.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Long-press to rearrange on his phone">
    On his Android phone, Rajesh long-presses a tile, taps "Rearrange
    widgets," and reorders his dashboard the same way there — his phone
    keeps its own copy of the layout, separate from the counter PC.
  </Step>
</Steps>
