title: "Creating an invoice"
description: "Create a customer invoice in BillBasket end to end — pick a customer, add items, apply tax and discounts, take payment, and print or share the bill."
---

An invoice is the core of daily billing in BillBasket — everything else
(adding items, discounts, tax, payment, printing) happens inside one. Every
invoice you save is stored permanently, so you can always reopen, edit, or
reprint it later from your invoice list.

## The pieces of an invoice

- **Customer** (optional) — pick an existing one, add a new one on the
  spot, or leave it blank for a quick walk-in sale. See [Adding a
  customer](/customer-guide/parties/adding-a-customer).
- **Line items** — the products/services being sold. See [Adding
  products/services to a bill](/customer-guide/daily-billing/adding-items-to-a-bill).
- **Discounts and tax** — applied automatically per product, adjustable per
  line or for the whole bill. See [Applying discounts &
  tax](/customer-guide/daily-billing/discounts-and-tax).
- **Payment** — cash, card, UPI, credit, or split across methods. See
  [Taking payment](/customer-guide/daily-billing/taking-payment).
- **Print/share** — receipt, label, PDF, or a direct share link. See
  [Printing & sharing bills](/customer-guide/daily-billing/printing-and-sharing).

## How the pieces depend on each other

Picking a customer isn't required to start a bill, but it unlocks two
things that don't apply to a walk-in sale: **credit** as a payment method
(see [Credit accounts](/customer-guide/parties/credit-accounts)), and,
where the customer has more than one registered tax number, a **tax
branch** picker at billing time (see [Per-customer tax
branches](/customer-guide/parties/per-customer-tax)). The invoice's
final total is what payment is collected against — so any discount or tax
change you make has to happen before you take payment, not after.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Start a new invoice">
    From the dashboard or the Billing tab, tap **New Invoice**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pick a customer, or skip it for a walk-in sale">
    Search for an existing customer or add a new one on the spot. You can
    also bill without picking a customer for quick walk-in sales.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add products or services">
    Search by name, category, or scan a barcode to add items to the bill.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Apply discounts and tax">
    Adjust quantity, price, or discount per line, or apply a bill-level
    discount. Tax is calculated automatically based on your product and
    customer tax settings.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Take payment">
    Choose cash, card, UPI, credit, or split the payment across methods.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Print or share the bill">
    Print to a receipt/label printer, save as PDF, or share directly with
    the customer.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Every invoice is saved the moment you save it — you can reopen, edit, or
  reprint it later from your invoice list.
</Tip>
