---
title: "Printing & sharing bills (PDF, label printing)"
description: "Print BillBasket invoices to thermal, A4, or label printers, save them as PDF, and share bills with customers over WhatsApp, email, or SMS."
---

Once an invoice is complete, you can print it, save it as a PDF, or share
it directly with the customer — all from the same invoice screen, and any
past invoice can be reprinted or re-shared later without recreating the
bill.

## Understanding the fields

- **Print** — sends the invoice to whichever printer is connected: a
  Bluetooth receipt/label printer on phone, or a regular Windows printer
  on desktop. See [Hardware & connectivity
  settings](/customer-guide/settings/hardware-and-connectivity) for
  how printers are paired/installed.
- **Label padding (horizontal/vertical)** — if a label is printing
  off-center on your label stock, this is adjusted from printer settings,
  not from the invoice screen itself — it's a one-time setup per printer,
  not something you change per bill.
- **Save as PDF** — generates a PDF copy on the device, independent of
  whether a printer is even connected.
- **Share channel** — WhatsApp, e-mail, or SMS. Only the channels turned
  on in Settings → Notifications appear here, and whichever one is marked
  **default** is offered first — see [Notifications
  settings](/customer-guide/settings/notifications). "Copy to
  clipboard" is always available regardless of what's turned on, as a
  fallback for any other app.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Print to a connected receipt or label printer">
    From the finished invoice, tap Print — it goes straight to whichever
    printer is paired/installed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save as PDF">
    Tap Save as PDF to keep a copy on the device, whether or not a
    printer is connected.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Share with the customer">
    Tap Share and pick a channel — whichever is set as default is offered
    first, or pick another enabled channel instead.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reprint or re-share a past invoice">
    Open any past invoice from the invoice list and print or share it
    again — you don't need to recreate the bill.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  If labels are printing off-center, adjust horizontal/vertical padding
  under printer settings rather than on the invoice screen — see
  [Hardware & connectivity settings](/customer-guide/settings/hardware-and-connectivity).
</Note>
