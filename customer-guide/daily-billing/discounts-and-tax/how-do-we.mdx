<Steps>
  <Step title="Apply a discount to one line">
    Tap a line item on the bill and enter a discount for just that
    product/service.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Apply a discount to the whole bill">
    Open the bill-level discount field and enter either a flat amount or
    a percentage — switching between the two keeps both views in sync, so
    you can check the total either way before confirming.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review the tax breakdown">
    Check the tax shown per line and the total before finishing the sale
    — this reflects each product's own tax rate, not a customer-level
    override.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Adjust a line's tax slab for a one-off exception">
    If a specific sale needs different tax treatment, change the tax slab
    on that invoice line only — this doesn't change the product's own
    saved tax rate for future sales.
  </Step>
</Steps>
