While billing, you can add items to the invoice in three ways:

- **Search** by product/service name, SKU, or category.
- **Scan a barcode** using your phone camera, a connected desktop webcam, or
  a hardware barcode scanner — see [Barcode
  scanning](/customer-guide/products-and-services/barcode-scanning).
- **Browse** your product/service list grouped by category.

Each line item shows quantity, unit price, unit of measure, and any per-item
discount. Tap a line to edit its quantity, price, or discount, or to remove
it from the bill.

<Note>
  If an item is low or out of stock, BillBasket flags it on the line so you
  know before you finish the sale.
</Note>
