title: "Adding products/services to a bill"
description: "Add products and services to a BillBasket invoice by searching your catalog, browsing categories, or scanning a barcode from the camera or a scanner."
---

While billing, you can add items to the invoice in three ways:

- **Search** by product/service name, SKU, or category.
- **Scan a barcode** using your phone camera, a connected desktop webcam,
  or a hardware barcode scanner — see [Barcode
  scanning](/customer-guide/products-and-services/barcode-scanning/explainer).
- **Browse** your product/service list grouped by category.

Each line item shows quantity, unit price, unit of measure, and any
per-item discount. Tap a line to edit its quantity, price, or discount, or
to remove it from the bill.

## Understanding the fields

- **Quantity** — how many units of that line are being sold. For a
  serial-tracked product, quantity is driven by how many serials you pick
  rather than typed directly — see [Tracking serial
  numbers](/customer-guide/products-and-services/serial-number-tracking/explainer).
- **Unit price** — defaults to the product/service's set price, but can be
  overridden per line for a one-off sale price.
- **Unit of measure** — shown next to quantity so it's clear whether
  you're billing by piece, kg, box, etc. — see [Units of
  measure](/customer-guide/products-and-services/units-of-measure/explainer).
- **Per-line discount** — a flat or percentage reduction applied to just
  this line, separate from any bill-level discount. See [Applying
  discounts & tax](/customer-guide/daily-billing/discounts-and-tax/explainer)
  for how line and bill discounts combine.

<Note>
  If an item is low or out of stock, BillBasket flags it on the line so
  you know before you finish the sale.
</Note>
