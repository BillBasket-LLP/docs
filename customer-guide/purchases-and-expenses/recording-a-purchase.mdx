---
title: "Recording a purchase"
description: "Record purchases from suppliers in BillBasket to update stock levels, track supplier dues, and account for landed cost against each product you sell."
---

Record a purchase from **Purchases → New Purchase**, pick the supplier and
items, and BillBasket adds the stock to your inventory and updates the
supplier's account if bought on credit.

## Try it: Sharma Kirana Store

<Steps>
  <Step title="A delivery arrives from Bansal Wholesale Traders">
    Bansal Wholesale Traders delivers a fresh batch of Tata Salt 1kg and
    Parle-G Biscuits to Sharma Kirana Store. Rajesh Sharma opens
    **Purchases → New Purchase** to log it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pick the supplier and items">
    He selects "Bansal Wholesale Traders" as the supplier, then adds Tata
    Salt 1kg and Parle-G Biscuits with the quantities and purchase price
    from the delivery invoice.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Stock updates automatically">
    Once saved, BillBasket adds the new quantities to inventory right
    away, ready to sell.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Track the supplier due if bought on credit">
    Since Rajesh took this delivery on credit, BillBasket updates Bansal
    Wholesale Traders' account with the amount owed, so he can track and
    settle it later.
  </Step>
</Steps>
